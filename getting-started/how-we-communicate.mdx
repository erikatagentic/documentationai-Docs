---
title: "How We Communicate"
---

We keep things simple. No unnecessary meetings, no fluff. Here's exactly how communication works.

## Weekly Updates

You'll get a written campaign update every week covering:
- Connection rates and reply rates
- Hot leads that need your follow-up
- What we recommend changing (if anything)
- Next steps

If a lead replies and needs your attention, we flag it with their name and what they said. You won't miss a warm prospect.

## How We Share Recommendations

When we think something should change (new messaging angle, different targeting, expanded lead list), we send a detailed email explaining the data behind the recommendation and the proposed change. You approve or suggest edits via email. We don't make big pivots without your sign-off.

## Response Times

We typically reply within 24 hours on business days. If something is urgent (like a LinkedIn account issue), we flag it the same day.

## When We Schedule Calls

Most communication happens over email. We don't schedule recurring calls unless you want them. If something complex comes up that's easier to talk through, we'll suggest a quick call. Otherwise, email keeps things efficient and documented.

## What We Need From You

- **Reply to hot lead flags within 48 hours.** Prospects go cold fast. When we tell you someone's interested, that's your window.
- **Tell us when you close a deal from a campaign lead.** We track ROI and use it to sharpen your targeting.
- **Flag any changes to your ICP, offer, or target market immediately.** If your business shifts, we need to know so campaigns stay relevant.
