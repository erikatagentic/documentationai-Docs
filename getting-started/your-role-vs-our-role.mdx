---
title: "Your Role vs. Our Role"
---

Clear expectations make everything work better. Here's exactly what we handle and what's on you.

## What We Do

- Research your ideal customer profile and build targeted lead lists
- Write your connection requests and follow-up sequences
- Set up, launch, and monitor your campaigns daily
- Optimize messaging based on performance data (A/B tests, copy refreshes)
- Send you weekly reports with metrics, hot leads, and recommendations
- Manage your LinkedIn sender profiles and account health
- Flag hot leads that need your follow-up
- Provide dedicated account manager support via email and Slack

## What You Do

- **Respond to leads when they reply.** This is the most important thing. When a prospect shows interest, that conversation is yours. We surface the opportunity, you close it.
- **Review and approve messaging before launch.** We write the copy, but you know your business best. Your feedback makes the messaging stronger.
- **Tell us when something changes.** New product, different target market, updated pricing, seasonal shift. We can't optimize campaigns around information we don't have.
- **Report wins.** When a campaign lead turns into a meeting or a deal, let us know. We use this data to refine what's working.

## What We Don't Do

- **We don't respond to prospects on your behalf.** When someone replies to your outreach, that's your conversation to continue. We can coach you on how to reply, but we don't send messages as you.
- **We don't guarantee a specific number of meetings.** We share benchmarks, work to exceed them, and optimize relentlessly. But LinkedIn outreach depends on your offer, your market, and your follow-up speed.
- **We don't manage your LinkedIn content or run paid ads.** Our focus is outbound outreach: connection requests, follow-up sequences, and lead generation.

If you want help managing replies and booking meetings directly, ask your account manager about the Appointment Setting add-on.
