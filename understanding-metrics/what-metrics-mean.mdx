---
title: "What Your Campaign Metrics Mean"
---

Your weekly updates include several metrics. Here's what each one actually measures and what "good" looks like.

## Connection Rate

**What it is:** The percentage of people who accept your LinkedIn connection request out of everyone who received one.

**Example:** You sent 100 connection requests, 22 people accepted. That's a 22% connection rate.

**What good looks like:**

| Range | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| Below 15% | Something's off. Targeting, profile quality, or your connection note needs a look. |
| 15-25% | Healthy. This is where most well-targeted campaigns land. |
| 25-40% | Strong. Your targeting and profile are working well together. |
| Above 40% | Excellent. We'll look at scaling volume here. |

## Reply Rate

**What it is:** The percentage of people who replied to any of your follow-up messages after connecting.

**Example:** 50 people connected with you, 8 replied. That's a 16% reply rate.

**What good looks like:**

| Range | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| Below 5% | Messaging needs a rework. |
| 5-10% | Below average, but fixable with copy tweaks. |
| 10-20% | Healthy. Most optimized campaigns sit here. |
| Above 20% | Excellent. Something is really clicking. |

**Important:** Reply rate includes all replies, positive and negative. A 15% reply rate where 40% of replies are interested is better than a 25% reply rate where most replies are "not interested."

## Positive Reply Ratio

**What it is:** The percentage of replies that show genuine interest (asking questions, requesting info, open to a call) vs. negative responses (not interested, wrong fit, opt-outs).

**Benchmark:** 30-50% positive is healthy. Above 50% is excellent.

## Weekly Send Volume

**What it is:** How many connection requests went out in a given week.

| Range | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| Below 20 | Account may be throttled or paused |
| 20-50 | Low volume, may indicate list exhaustion |
| 50-100 | Normal operating range |
| 100+ | High volume, we monitor closely for LinkedIn restrictions |

## Messages Sent

**What it is:** The total number of follow-up messages sent to people who accepted your connection. This is separate from connection requests. If this number is low on a new campaign, it just means your accepted connections haven't hit the follow-up timing window yet. Give it a week.
