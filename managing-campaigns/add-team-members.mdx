---
title: "How to Add Team Members"
---

You can invite colleagues to view your campaign dashboard and conversations. Here's how it works.

## Adding Someone

Let your account manager know who you'd like to add. We'll need:
- Their email address
- What level of access they should have (view-only or full access)

We handle the invitation from our side to make sure permissions are set correctly.

## Permission Levels

**View-only:** Can see campaign stats, conversations, and lead lists. Can't make changes to campaigns or settings. Good for executives or team members who want visibility without the ability to accidentally change something.

**Full access:** Can see everything and interact with the dashboard. Good for sales team members who need to monitor replies and manage conversations.

## What Team Members Can See

- All active campaigns and their stats
- Conversations in the Unified Inbox
- Lead lists and prospect details
- Campaign performance metrics

## What Team Members Can't Do

Regardless of permission level, team members can't:
- Pause or resume campaigns (we manage this)
- Change messaging sequences (this goes through your account manager)
- Modify LinkedIn sender account settings
- Adjust daily sending limits

## Removing Someone

If a team member leaves your company or no longer needs access, let your account manager know and we'll remove them the same day.
