---
title: "How to Add Team Members"
---

You can invite colleagues to view your campaign dashboard and conversations. Here's how it works.

## Adding Someone

Let your account manager know who you'd like to add. We'll need:
- Their email address
- What level of access they should have

We handle the invitation from our side to make sure permissions are set correctly.

## Permission Levels

The platform uses two roles:

**Admin:** Full access to everything, including billing, workspace settings, and member management. This is typically reserved for account owners and your account manager.

**Member:** Customizable access on a per-feature basis. We can set View or Manage permissions for each area of the dashboard, including campaigns, conversations, lead lists, sender accounts, and integrations. Most team members get added as Members with permissions tailored to what they actually need.

When you request a new team member, just let us know what they should be able to see and do. We'll configure their permissions accordingly.

## What Team Members Can See

Depending on their permissions, team members can access:
- Active campaigns and their stats
- Conversations in the Unified Inbox
- Lead lists and prospect details
- Campaign performance metrics

## Campaign Changes Go Through Your Account Manager

Your account is configured so that campaign changes go through your account manager. This includes things like pausing or resuming campaigns, updating messaging sequences, modifying sender account settings, and adjusting daily sending limits. This keeps everything coordinated and prevents accidental changes that could affect performance.

## Removing Someone

If a team member leaves your company or no longer needs access, let your account manager know and we'll remove them the same day.
