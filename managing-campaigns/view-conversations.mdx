---
title: "How to View Your Conversations"
---

When prospects reply to your outreach, their messages show up in your Unified Inbox (we call it "Unibox"). Here's how to find and manage your conversations.

## Finding the Inbox

Log into your dashboard and click "Unibox" in the left sidebar. This pulls all LinkedIn conversations from all your sender accounts into one place.

## What You'll See

The inbox is split into three panels: your conversation list on the left, the active chat in the center, and lead details on the right. Each conversation shows:
- The prospect's name and LinkedIn headline
- The full message thread (your outreach + their replies)
- Which sender account the conversation is tied to
- Timestamps for each message

## Filtering and Searching

You can search by prospect name or keyword using the search bar at the top. Beyond that, there are several filters to narrow things down:
- **Sender account** -- see conversations for a specific LinkedIn profile
- **Campaign** -- filter by which campaign generated the conversation
- **Tags** -- custom labels you or your AM have applied to leads
- **Answered/Unanswered** -- quickly find conversations that still need a response
- **Sort by** -- newest messages or unread first

## Using Tags

Tags let you organize conversations by category. Your account manager can create tags (like "Hot Lead", "Meeting Booked", or "Follow Up Later") and assign them to leads. You can filter the inbox by tag to see just the conversations that matter to you right now.

## Responding to Prospects

When you see a reply that needs a response, go directly to LinkedIn to continue the conversation. Your responses should come from LinkedIn itself so the conversation feels natural and personal.

**Pro tip:** Check your inbox at least once a day. Prospects who reply within 24-48 hours are warm. After 72 hours, interest drops off sharply.

## Unread vs. Read

New messages show as unread until you open them. We also flag hot leads in your weekly update, so even if you miss something in the inbox, your account manager will catch it.

## Multiple Sender Accounts

If you have 2+ LinkedIn profiles running, all conversations from every sender appear in the same inbox. Use the sender filter to see conversations per profile if needed.
