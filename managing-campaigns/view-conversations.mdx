---
title: "How to View Your Conversations"
---

When prospects reply to your outreach, their messages show up in your Unified Inbox (we call it "Unibox"). Here's how to find and manage your conversations.

## Finding the Inbox

Log into your dashboard and click "Unibox" in the left sidebar. This pulls all LinkedIn conversations from all your sender accounts into one place.

## What You'll See

Each conversation shows:
- The prospect's name and LinkedIn headline
- The full message thread (your outreach + their replies)
- Which sender account the conversation is tied to
- Timestamps for each message

You can filter conversations by sender account if you have multiple profiles running, and search by prospect name or keyword.

## Responding to Prospects

When you see a reply that needs a response, go directly to LinkedIn to continue the conversation. The inbox is for monitoring, not for sending replies. Your responses should come from LinkedIn itself so the conversation feels natural and personal.

**Pro tip:** Check your inbox at least once a day. Prospects who reply within 24-48 hours are warm. After 72 hours, interest drops off sharply.

## Unread vs. Read

New messages show as unread until you open them. We also flag hot leads in your weekly update, so even if you miss something in the inbox, your account manager will catch it.

## Multiple Sender Accounts

If you're on a Growth or Scale package with 2+ LinkedIn profiles, all conversations from every sender appear in the same inbox. Use the account filter to see conversations per sender if needed.
