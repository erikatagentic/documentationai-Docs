---
title: "Campaign Pausing and Resuming"
---

Sometimes campaigns need to be paused. Here's when that happens and what it means for your outreach.

## When We Pause a Campaign

**Account health issue:** If LinkedIn flags your sender account (usually because of high daily volume or unusual activity), we pause the campaign immediately and reduce limits. This protects your account from getting restricted. We'll let you know when it's safe to resume.

**Lead list running low:** When a campaign's lead list drops below a usable threshold, we pause it to avoid sending to exhausted segments. We'll recommend refreshing the list or building a new one.

**At your request:** If you need to pause for any reason (vacation, seasonal slowdown, business pivot), just tell your account manager. We can pause individual campaigns or everything at once.

## What Happens When a Campaign is Paused

- No new connection requests go out
- No new follow-up messages are sent
- Conversations that are already in progress continue (prospects can still reply to messages you've already sent)
- Your lead list and campaign settings stay intact
- Metrics are preserved

Pausing doesn't lose any data. Everything picks up where it left off when you resume.

## Resuming a Campaign

When you're ready to resume, let your account manager know. We typically restart within 24 hours. After resuming, the campaign may take a few minutes to become fully active again (you might see a "Starting" status before it switches back to "Ongoing"). If you were paused due to an account health issue, we may recommend starting at a lower daily volume and ramping back up over a few days.

## Temporary vs. Permanent Pauses

If you're pausing for a short break (a week or two), we keep everything as-is. If you're pausing for more than a month, we may recommend refreshing your lead list and messaging before relaunching, since the market moves and stale lists underperform.

## Can I Pause or Resume Campaigns Myself?

Campaign management goes through your account manager. This keeps things coordinated so we don't accidentally overlap campaigns, exceed safe sending limits, or resume something that needs a list refresh first.
