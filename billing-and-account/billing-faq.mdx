---
title: "Billing FAQ"
---

Straight answers to the most common billing questions.

## When do I get charged?

Your billing cycle starts when your first invoice is paid. After that, you're charged on the same date each month. For example, if you paid your first invoice on March 12, your next charge hits April 12.

## Is there a contract?

No. All plans are month-to-month. No setup fees, no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime.

## How do I cancel?

Email your account manager. We'll confirm your cancellation and let you know when your current billing period ends. Your campaigns continue running until that date. We don't prorate or refund partial months, but we don't charge you past your cycle end either.

## What happens to my campaigns when I cancel?

Campaigns keep running until your billing period ends. On that date, we pause everything. Your data (conversations, lead lists, campaign history) stays in the system, so if you come back later, we can pick up where you left off.

## Can I pause instead of canceling?

Yes. If you need a break (vacation, seasonal slowdown, budget reasons), tell your account manager. We can pause campaigns while keeping your account active. Depending on how long the pause is, billing may or may not continue. Your AM will walk you through the options.

## Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately (we add profiles, increase limits, etc.). Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Talk to your account manager about what makes sense.

## I got charged but my campaigns haven't launched yet

During onboarding, there's a setup period (typically 1-2 weeks) where we're building lead lists, writing messaging, and optimizing your sender profiles. Billing starts when you pay the first invoice, not when campaigns go live. We align the first billing date with your expected launch date whenever possible.
