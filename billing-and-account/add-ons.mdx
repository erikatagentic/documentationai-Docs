Two add-ons you can stack on top of any LinkedIn outreach package.

## Cold Email ($1,997/month)

If LinkedIn alone isn't enough volume, cold email adds a second channel. Here's what's included:

- 30,000+ emails sent per month
- Private sending infrastructure (dedicated IP, custom domains)
- Full domain and mailbox setup (SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured for you)
- Email warmup and deliverability monitoring
- Weekly inbox placement tests
- Done-for-you copywriting (separate from LinkedIn sequences)
- Lead sourcing and enrichment
- AI-personalized messaging
- Unified inbox for email replies
- Dedicated account manager
- Automatic lead notifications and routing

We handle everything: infrastructure, copy, sending, monitoring. You review the messaging and respond to interested replies, same as LinkedIn.

**Who it's for:** Companies that want to reach prospects who aren't active on LinkedIn, or who need higher volume than LinkedIn alone can deliver. Works well in industries where email is still the primary communication channel (finance, legal, healthcare).

## Appointment Setting ($397/month)

If you're getting replies from campaigns but don't have time to manage the back-and-forth, this add-on handles it for you.

- Dedicated appointment setter who responds to prospect replies
- Multi-channel follow-up (email + LinkedIn)
- ICP qualification before scheduling calls
- Calendar sync for instant scheduling
- Automated reminders and follow-ups
- No-show recovery and rescheduling
- Custom SLA response times
- Objection handling
- CRM integration and call notes
- Weekly reporting on conversations and bookings

**Who it's for:** Founders and sales leaders who are getting warm replies but can't respond fast enough, or whose team doesn't have the bandwidth to manage 10-20 active conversations per week.

## Can I Add These Later?

Yes. Most clients start with LinkedIn outreach alone and add cold email or appointment setting after the first 60-90 days once they see how the pipeline looks. Talk to your account manager when you're ready.
